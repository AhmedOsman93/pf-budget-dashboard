Key Terms & Definitions
Confirmed Savings Cuts that are already committed and locked in — contracts cancelled, vendors notified. These reduce the budget regardless of any other decisions.
Levers Optional further cuts that have not yet been committed. Toggle them on in the Decision Sheet to model the impact. All 6 levers are in Ahmed's Growth Marketing team.
Jan–Mar Consumed Budget already spent in the first three months of the year. This money is gone — it cannot be recovered or redirected.
Apr–Dec Remaining Budget available to spend from April through December, after confirmed savings are applied. April spend = Performance + App only. This is the budget still under our control.
Net Apr–Dec Budget The Apr–Dec budget after applying any active levers. This is what the team would actually spend if selected levers are executed.
Target Saving Selected The running total of all active savings — confirmed cuts plus any levers you've toggled on. The team's 2026 savings plan targets $9.4M.
Data notes: Growth Marketing (Performance, App) is assumed fully spent Apr–Dec. OOH, Spafax (from May), and Digital Branding are confirmed cancelled and reflected in confirmed savings. Spafax/Emirates, Creative Agency, Survey, B2B, PR, and Social/Content budget figures are sourced from the latest aligned marketing plan.
